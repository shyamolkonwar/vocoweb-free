# **Page: Authentication (Login / Sign Up)**

### **1. The Layout Architecture (Split Screen)**

Instead of a small box floating in the middle of a white screen, we split the viewport into two distinct halves:

* **Left Side (40% Width): The "Brand Wall"**
* **Background:** Deep Forest Green (`#064e3b` or your darkest brand shade).
* **Content:** A large meaningful visual, a testimonial, or a statistic. This reminds the user *why* they are logging in.
* **Visibility:** Hidden on Mobile (`hidden md:flex`).


* **Right Side (60% Width): The "Action Zone"**
* **Background:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`).
* **Content:** The Login Form, centered vertically and horizontally.
* **Navbar Fix:** This layout naturally fixes the navbar overlap because we will place the form inside a container with proper spacing.



---

### **2. The "Navbar Fix" (Technical Instruction)**

Since your Navbar is `fixed` (floating on top), it removes itself from the document flow, causing the login form to slide underneath it.

**The Fix:**
You must apply **Top Padding** to the container wrapping the login form, equal to the height of your navbar.

* **Tailwind Class:** `pt-24` (roughly 96px) or `pt-28` (112px).
* **Application:** Apply this *only* to the Right Side container.

#### **B. Right Panel (The Form)**

* **Alignment:** Flexbox Centered (`flex flex-col justify-center items-center`).
* **Container Width:** Max width `400px` (don't let the form stretch too wide).
* **Header:**
* **Title:** "Welcome back" (Bold, Slate-900).
* **Subtext:** "Manage your website from anywhere." (Slate-500).


* **Primary Action (The "Google" Button):**
* Since you are targeting mobile users, **"Continue with Google"** should be the massive, primary button.
* **Style:** White background, thin Grey border, Google Logo icon, text "Continue with Google".
* **Hover:** Slight shadow lift.


* **Divider:** A simple line with text "or email" in the middle.
* **Inputs:**
* Email & Password fields with rounded corners (`rounded-lg`) and a focus ring color of Green (`focus:ring-green-600`).


* **Submit Button:**
* **Text:** "Log In"
* **Style:** Solid Green background, full width.
