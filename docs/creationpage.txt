
# **Page: The Creation Studio**

### **1. The "App Shell" Layout (Fixing the Navbar)**

* **Background:** unlike the Landing Page (which is White/Green), the App Studio should be **Off-White / Light Grey** (`#F3F4F6` or `slate-50`). This makes the white "work area" pop.
* **Spacing Fix:** The main container must have **Top Padding** (`pt-24` or `pt-28`) to push content *below* the floating navbar.
* **Navbar State:** On this page, the Navbar should be **simplified**.
* *Show:* Logo (Left), User Profile (Right).
* *Hide:* "Features", "Pricing", "Get Started" links (distractions).



---

### **2. The "Progress Header"**

* **Location:** Directly below the Navbar, centered.
* **Visual:** A sleek, segmented progress bar.
* **Steps:**
1. **Identity** (Input)
2. **Preview** (Design)
3. **Launch** (Publish)


* **Style:**
* *Active Step:* Deep Green text + Solid Green line.
* *Inactive Step:* Grey text + Faint Grey line.



---

### **3. The "Focus Card" (The Main Interaction)**

Instead of loose text floating on the screen, wrap the entire interaction in a **Massive Central Card**.

* **Dimensions:** Max-width `800px`, centered horizontally.
* **Appearance:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`), Rounded Corners (`rounded-2xl`), Heavy Shadow (`shadow-xl`).
* **Animation:** The card should slide up slightly (`y: 20px -> 0`) when the page loads.

#### **Inside the Card: The Voice Interface**

This is the core of VocoWeb. It needs to look magical, not static.

* **Headline:** "Tell us about your business."
* **Subtext:** "Include your business name, location, and what you sell. You can speak in Hindi or English."
* **The "Mic" Button (Center Stage):**
* **Size:** Large (80px x 80px).
* **Color:** Gradient Green.
* **Icon:** A white microphone.
* **Animation:** When active/recording, add a **"Ripple" effect** (concentric circles fading out) to visualize that it is listening.


* **The Text Alternative:**
* Below the mic, a simple text link: *"Or type it manually"* which expands a clean text area.



---

### **4. The "Loading/Generating" State (Crucial)**

Once they submit, don't just show a spinner. Show **"Thinking Steps"** to make the AI feel smarter (and justify the 30-second wait).

* **Visual:** A checklist that ticks off automatically.
* [✓] *Analyzing Voice Note...*
* [✓] *Detecting Business Type (Bakery)...*
* [✓] *Writing SEO-friendly Content...*
* [  ] *Designing Layout...*
