=== VOCOWEB BRANDING GUIDELINES BY INDUSTRY ===

This file contains design guidelines for different industry verticals.
The AI should apply these guidelines based on the detected BUSINESS_TYPE.

================================================================================
HEALTHCARE (Dental, Medical, Clinics, Pharmacies)
================================================================================
VIBE: Trust, Clean, Professional
PRIMARY COLORS: #0d9488 (Teal), #0891b2 (Cyan), #0284c7 (Blue)
ACCENT COLORS: #f97316 (Orange for CTAs), #22c55e (Green for health)
FONTS: 
  - Heading: Inter (clean, professional)
  - Body: Inter
DESIGN PATTERNS:
  - Clean white backgrounds with subtle gradients
  - Rounded corners (rounded-xl) for approachability
  - Trust badges prominently displayed
  - Doctor/staff photos with professional backgrounds
MUST INCLUDE:
  - Certifications/Registrations
  - Hygiene/Safety protocols
  - Emergency contact numbers
  - Operating hours prominently displayed

================================================================================
FOOD & HOSPITALITY (Restaurants, Cafes, Bakeries, Catering)
================================================================================

FONTS:

  - Body: Inter
DESIGN PATTERNS:
  - Warm color gradients

MUST INCLUDE:

  - Delivery/Takeaway options
  - Operating hours
